About CyberShield

Who We Are

CyberShield is a student project created to
spread awareness about online theft, hacking,
and cyber safety.
Our goal is to educate users on now to identify scams,
protect personal data, and stay safe online

objective

  • To educate people about different types of online fraud
  • To promote safe online habits among students
  • To provide practical prevention tips and real examples
  • To encourage responsible use of technology
