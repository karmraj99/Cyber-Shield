how to protect yourself

Steps to help you stay safe on the internet
involve taking simple but important actions to protect
your personal data, keep your money and financial
accounts secure, and shield your identity from
hackers, scammers, and other online threats.

use strong password

  • don't use birthdays or name
  • use a mix of letter, number and symbols
  • never use the same password on all sites

avoid suspicious links and emails

  • never click unknown links in messages or emails
  • click the sender's email carefully
  • look for fake domains like "amaZ0n.com"

enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

  • always turn on otp or authentication apps
  • even if someone gets your password, they can't log in without your phone

don't share personal info

  • never share otps, pins, or password with anyone
  • avoid posting personal info publicly

use secure networks

  • avoid public Wi-Fi for payments or logins
  • use VPNs if possible

do

  • use strong passwords
  • log out from public pcs
  • report fake pages
  • use official apps

don't

  • share otps or pins
  • save passwords on shared devices
  • ignore suspicious messages
  • download cracked software
