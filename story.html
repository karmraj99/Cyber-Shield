Aman
The Job Offer Fraud
A company offered me a data entry job from home. Everything seemed legit — website, offer letter, and WhatsApp HR. They said I had to buy “software access” for ₹1,200 first. After I paid, they never replied again.
Arjun
The Phishing Email Trap
I got an email from what looked like my bank. It said, “Your account will be locked — verify now.” I clicked the link and entered my details. Within minutes, ₹5,000 was withdrawn from my account. It wasn’t the real bank site — just a lookalike page.
Riya
The Fake Shopping Website
Last year, I found a website selling branded shoes at 70% discount. It looked professional, had reviews, and even a support number. I ordered a pair and paid online. After a week, the site disappeared, and my number was blocked. That’s when I realized it was a fake e-commerce scam.
